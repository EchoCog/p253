### Pattern template

Flow Elements and Reservoirs provide essential structural and functional elements that support the healthy development and maintenance of complex systems.

**Physical** Bodies of water and flowing streams provide essential elements for both the ecological health of the land and the spiritual health of the people.

**Social** Resource flows and knowledge repositories provide essential elements for organizational health and collective learning.

**Conceptual** Knowledge repositories and information flows provide essential resources for intellectual development.

**Psychic** Emotional reservoirs and flowing states of consciousness provide essential resources for psychological health.
