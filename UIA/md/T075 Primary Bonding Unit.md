### Pattern template

Primary Bonding Unit provide essential structural and functional elements that support the healthy development and maintenance of complex systems.

**Physical** Housing should be designed to support different family structures and their changing needs over time.

**Social** Core organizational units should support diverse structures and evolving relationship patterns.

**Conceptual** Core knowledge communities should support diverse learning styles and evolving understanding.

**Psychic** Core psychological structures should support diverse aspects of self and evolving identity.
