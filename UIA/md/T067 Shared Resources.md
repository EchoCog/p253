### Pattern template

Shared Resources provide essential structural and functional elements that support the healthy development and maintenance of complex systems.

**Physical** Land held in common by communities provides essential space for collective activities and shared resources.

**Social** Shared resources and collective assets provide the foundation for community cooperation and mutual support.

**Conceptual** Shared conceptual frameworks provide the basis for collective understanding and communication.

**Psychic** Shared psychological resources provide the foundation for healthy relationships and community.
