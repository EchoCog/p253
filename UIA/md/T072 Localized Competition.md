### Pattern template

Localized Competition provide essential structural and functional elements that support the healthy development and maintenance of complex systems.

**Physical** Local sports facilities should be embedded within neighborhoods to serve the immediate community.

**Social** Local competitive activities build community bonds and provide healthy outlets for group energy.

**Conceptual** Intellectual competitions and debates build community and test ideas in healthy ways.

**Psychic** Healthy psychological competition builds self-confidence and tests personal boundaries.
