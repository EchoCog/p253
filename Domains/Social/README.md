# Social Environment Patterns

## Description
This folder contains patterns for **organizing social groups, organizations, and networks**. These patterns address collective human organization and community structures.

## Purpose
- Guide the formation and governance of social groups
- Address organizational structure and dynamics
- Create healthy community relationships and networks
- Balance individual and collective needs

## Pattern Structure
Each pattern in this domain describes:
- **Social Problem**: Organizational or community challenge
- **Group Context**: The social setting and stakeholder relationships
- **Organizational Solution**: Principles for structuring social systems
- **Community Examples**: Applications in groups, institutions, and networks

## Key Characteristics
- **Collective focus**: Addresses group dynamics and social systems
- **Organizational principles**: Guides structure and governance
- **Network relationships**: Manages connections between groups
- **Human cooperation**: Facilitates collaboration and coordination

## Examples
- Networks of organizations as independent spheres of influence
- Distribution of group sizes across functional domains
- Interpenetration of formal and informal organizational modes
- Networked relationships linking central and decentralized groups

## Source
Social adaptations from the UIA 5-fold Pattern Language collection, focusing on organizational and community applications.
